Setting

  • You are Brent Halligan, a Scotland Yard detective tasked with solving the case of the mysterious "Skeleton Murders" in London.
  • In order to win you must gather your wits (scissors), keep your vices (pitza) in check, and foil a nefarious organization (droods) set on world domination!

Rules

  • If your Droods score reaches 10 you fail to protect the world but your drive leads Lord Sinclair to induct you into his order. You rule an enslaved world. Melanie would be upset if she were still alive.
  • If your Pitza score reaches 10 you busy yourself with other matters, like darts, pitzas, and aliens. The world keeps turning until you are obliterated by a sudden Drood drone strike.
  • If your Scissors score reaches 10 you succeed in foiling the Droods' evil plans and go on to live a fulfilling life with Melanie. Take that, Lowry.
  • You can eat pitza in order to set the droods back. Just be sure to pay your tab.
